Soft Whitelist

To join the server, we have implemented a soft whitelist system to maintain the quality of our player base. Being on the soft whitelist indicates your intent to join the community; however, it does not guarantee a permanent place. Staff members reserve the right to remove players from the soft whitelist if deemed necessary to uphold the standards of roleplay within the community.


Whitelist

The whitelist is granted only through an application process or by specific members of the team. Whitelist status can only be revoked if the player has been found guilty of repeated rule violations, requiring staff intervention to address the breaches.


Whitelist applications can only be found in the discord after being Soft Whitelisted
