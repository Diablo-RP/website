Roles Overview

Diablo RP offers three main roles for content creators: Streamer, Content Creator, and Endorsed. Each role comes with unique perks and requirements designed to enhance your experience and involvement in the community. Explore the details below and apply for the role that suits you best.

Streamer

The Streamer role allows casual streamers to post in the live-now channel. This role has no strict requirements other than following server rules and maintaining a positive presence in the community.

Content Creator

The Content Creator role is ideal for those ready to take the next step as a streamer. This role offers various perks but comes with specific requirements, including streaming hours and active promotion of the server.

Endorsed

The Endorsed role offers premium benefits, including custom in-game objects, priority queuing, and staff-supported storyline enhancements. This role is for exceptional creators who contribute significantly to the server's growth.
